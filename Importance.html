Women's Safety

Crimes against women are a blot on our conscience. The protection of women from all forms of abuse and oppression is now a national duty. The status of women in India needs the continuous attention of the government, civil society, and role models who can shape public opinion. Everyone must take an active part in ensuring women's safety. We must stand together to fight violence against women.

Why is Self-Defense Important?

Every day, we hear stories of women being victimized—through sexual violence, random attacks, or domestic abuse. In such a world, self-defense is a vital skill that empowers women to protect themselves.

Some key reasons why self-defense matters:

  • Safety: To protect against any form of physical or verbal assault.
  • Confidence: Helps analyze and react to dangerous situations with clarity.
  • Empowerment: Enables women to take charge of their safety and decisions.
  • Discipline: Enhances self-control, awareness, and mental clarity.
  • Reduces Dependency: Encourages independence in travel and daily life.
  • Health and Fitness: Improves both physical strength and mental well-being.
  • Reduces Discrimination/Sexism: Encourages women to stand up against injustice.
